SUBMISSIONS

Submitters should emphasize writing or artwork pertaining to darker themes and situations within the Ozarks and send their file to feralozarkiangothic@gmail.com. Any format is welcome. Editors reserve the right the talk shit, edit-ta-death, or flat out refuse your chickenscratch.
